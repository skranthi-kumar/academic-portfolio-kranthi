About

Researcher in AI and Cloud Computing

I am a Pre final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science and Engineering (Cloud Computing) at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai. My research focuses on Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and AI-driven solutions for societal challenges.

I lead Vision 2047: AI Upskilling Project, approved by the District Collector, Ananthapuramu, to train employees in AI. My work on Tejas Vision and drought prediction earned the Best Paper Award at ICDSBS 2025. As Executive Secretary of Qwiklabs Developer Club SRMIST, I have organized cloud-focused workshops.
